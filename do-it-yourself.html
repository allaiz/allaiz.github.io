Job seeking? Well if you want something done right...

…do it yourself. There are hundreds of applicants for every job right now. So don’t wait for someone to employ you. Employ yourself instead.

You have skills, talent, and experience. You have (some) ideas and (some) resources. All you need is a team; some talent to help so you can be competitive. And right now there is so much talent unemployed, that it’s as if there were diamonds lying in the street.

And you need to work with that talent in a business structure that can ride out these tough conditions without being bankrupted by overheads. Setting up a Self Employer Alliance could help you attract top-notch collaborators to launch a business, while eliminating fixed wage expenses that could sink a new business trying to get established in this market.

That might be your fastest way out of unemployment, and leave you in a better position than you were in when this all started.

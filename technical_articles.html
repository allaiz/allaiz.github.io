Building in public


I am working to make Allaiz.com into a useful tool for Self Employers to run their businesses and to create Joint Ventures with other Self Employers. Much of this work involves the technical details of creating a SaaS app, such as web and app design, software development, and system administration.

To help other Self Employers who want to create their own SaaS apps too, I am recording my progress here in a series of technical articles that explain what goes on "under the hood" of Allaiz.com, and that link to the resources you would need to do the same thing.

Enjoy! 😀
